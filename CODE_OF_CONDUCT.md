# Code of Conduct

PCIS is a project about integrity — in systems, and in how people work together
on it. Everyone who takes part deserves a respectful experience, free of
harassment, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, considerate, and constructive.
- Welcome differing viewpoints; give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Keep the focus on what's best for the project and the people who rely on it.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning remarks.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or of others' contributions.

## Scope

This applies across all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests,
and any project communication — and whenever someone is representing the project
in public.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to **idwty@proton.me**. Maintainers will review in good faith
and may edit, remove, or reject contributions — or restrict participation — that
violate this standard. Enforcement aims to be proportionate and transparent.
